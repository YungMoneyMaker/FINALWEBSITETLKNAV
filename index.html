ABOUT THE HALLWAY NAVIGATOR

The TLK Nav project was developed by a team of coding rookies who wanted to collaborate and handle the issue people at Thomas Laird Kennedy Secondary School may encounter regarding movement and direction around the school premises. The team first took into consideration the question: "What are the most efficient ways to maximize student usage of our working product and provide adequate satisfaction given the skill level of the current team?" After using this question as a base for our issue which needed problem-solving we brought our most prevalent programming and coding skills to provide public access to our working product. As of now, people may find their ways throughout the school as they please.
